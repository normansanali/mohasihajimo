Bradley Martyn Started as a Fitness Expert

Bradley Martyn started his thriving fitness career as a 14-year-old in 2003. With the aid of constant gym training, he gradually achieved his enviable powerlifting skills and fascinating physique. Barely three years later, in January 2006, the youngster established the fitness program known as BMFit. The program offers clients training routines and guidance on meal plans plus nutritional supplemental.

He later co-founded fitness centers such as AlphaCre8tivedesign and Zoo Culture Gym (May 2017). Bradley owns gym-related equipment such as Origin Supplements, he also established fitness equipment and clothing apparel called BMFIT Gear’.

He Follows a Crazy Workout Routine

Bradley Grew His Fitness Career Via YouTube

On the 20th of August 2014, Bradley stepped foot into the internet space by launching his self-titled YouTube channel.
