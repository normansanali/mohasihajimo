Jennifer Cudjoe NWSL: Everything On Her Parents &amp; Family

NWSL player Jennifer Cudjoe was born to Ghanaian parents in Accra, Ghana. Continue reading for more info about Jennifer.

Jennifer Cudjoe is a professional soccer player who represents NJ/NY Gotham FC in the National Women’s Soccer League as a midfielder (NWSL).

In addition, she also gained popularity as a midfielder of “Sky Blue FC” and is best for this title.

The 27-year-old athlete had agreed to a one-year deal with Sky Blue FC for the 2020 NWSL Challenge Cup. Moreover, on June 30, 2020, she made her NWSL debut.

Cudjoe was selected in the 2020 NWSL Expansion Draft by Racing Louisville FC; however, she was quickly acquired by Sky Blue in a trade.

She has also been honored as the “Ghanaian Female Footballer of the Year: 2015”.
