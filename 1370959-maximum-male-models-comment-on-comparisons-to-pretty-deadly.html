Maximum Male Models Comment On Comparisons To Pretty Deadly

The Maximum Male Models (Mace and Mansoor) are known for being extremely outlandish and unafraid to be flamboyant. The duo has been compared to Pretty Deadly (Elton Prince and Kit Wilson) in that regard, and they’re aware of the similarities.

Speaking to Alistair McGeorge of Metro, the Maximum Male Models commented on being compared to Pretty Deadly.

“It’s funny, because you see how people think similarly, have the same ideas,” Mace said. “Even the “tip touch”, we had no idea that was something they used to do.

Mansoor also chimed in that the duo do watch Pretty Deadly now, and that sometimes they’ll see Elton Prince and Kit Wilson do something and think to themselves how they would do the same.

“We actually watch them,” Mansoor said, before adding that they would “love to work with those guys!”

Mansoor also spoke highly of Elton and Prince, as he complimented the former NXT tag Team Champions.

“I think they’re a phenomenal tag team, I think they’re awesome guys as well,” Mansoor said. “I’ve seen them working at the PC – they’ve got a great attitude, phenomenal character performers, phenomenal performers too.”

Recently, Pretty Deadly was drafted to SmackDown as part of the 2023 WWE Draft.
