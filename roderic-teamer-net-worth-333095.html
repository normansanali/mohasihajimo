About

NFL safety that was originally signed by the Los Angeles Rams as an undrafted free agent in 2021. He was later signed by the Las Vegas Raiders in June 2021. He has also played for the Indianapolis Colts during his NFL career.

Previously, he played college football at Tulane University. Following his 2018 season, he was named a member of the second team All-AAC.

He made his official NFL debut while playing against the Houston Texans during week three of the 2022 regular season. He recorded 30 tackles with one interception and one sack in seven games played during his rookie season.

He was born and raised in New Orleans, Louisiana. He is one of three siblings. He has a son named Azazel in 2019.

He played alongside Chandler Jones as a member of the Raiders during the 2022 NFL season.
