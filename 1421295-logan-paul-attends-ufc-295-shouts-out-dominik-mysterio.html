Logan Paul Attends UFC 295, Shouts Out Dominik Mysterio

Logan Paul gave Dominik Mysterio a shout-out at UFC 295.

WWE United States Champion Logan Paul took in the action at UFC 295 on November 11. During the broadcast, he was shown in the crowd. Paul held up his phone, which had a message that read, “Shout out to Dominick Mysterio” on it. Though Paul spelled the NXT North American Champion’s name wrong, he at least acknowledged the Judgment Day member on a major platform.
