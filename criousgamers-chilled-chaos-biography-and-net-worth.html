CriousGamers (Chilled Chaos) Biography And Net Worth

Chilled Chaos known also by his first name Anthony, is an American internet sensation and YouTube personality best recognized for playing games on his channel ‘ChilledChaos’ while doing fun commentary.

Chilled Chaos was born on May 30, 1991, in New York in the United States of America.

He is known as a former member of the gaming group known as ‘The Creatures’.

He is a graduate of the Hofstra University, a private University in New York City in the United States.

Anthony created his channel ChilledChaos in October 2008 and the channel currently has up to 1.42 million subscribers.

To date, this channel has been regularly updated with numerous gameplay videos related to popular video games like The Sims 3, Fallout 4, Mario Kart, Town of Salem, Duck Game, and many more.

Anthony is currently in a relationship with, Jess, who appears in a number of his videos. They got engaged in February 2018.

CriousGamers Net Worth

Chilled Chaos has some wealth to his name. As of now, Chilled Chaos has an estimated net worth valued at $2.5 million.
