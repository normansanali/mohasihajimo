Chris Jericho Owns Hater For Telling Him How To Run His Podcast

Chris Jericho does what he wants because that’s the kind of perk that comes from being the first-ever Undisputed Champion.

He recently had a guest on Talk Is Jericho who let loose about a vast array of conspiracy theories from government control to the flat earth theory. Jericho let him talk and make his points and kept his opinion to himself.

One fan didn’t seem to appreciate the rhetoric being spoken about on Talk Is Jericho and decided to let Y2J know about it. But the reply he received was less than receptive from Chris Jericho.

Therefore, the next time you plan on telling Jericho what to do with his podcast you might need to keep it to yourself because he’s obviously not looking for any pointers.
