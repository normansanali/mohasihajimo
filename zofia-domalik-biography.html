Zofia Domalik Bio (Wiki)

Who is Zofia Domalik?

Zofia Domalik is a popular Polish actress. Domalik is well-known for her appearance in the movie All for My Mother (2019), Bartkowiak (2021), and Kiss, Kiss! (2022).

Zofia Domalik- Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Zofia Domalik was born on October 10, 1915, in Warsaw, Poland. As of 2023, she will be celebrating her 28th birthday. Likewise, Zofia’s Zodiac sign is Libra and her ethnicity is Polish.

Zofia Domalik is mostly known as the daughter of the famous actress Ewa Telega (Mother) and Director Andrzej Domalik (Father). It is not known whether she has any siblings or not.

Domalik attended and graduated from Theater Academy in Warsaw in 2019.

Zofia Domalik- Professional Life, Careers

Zofia Domalik started her acting scene when she was 12 years old and participates in Dramatic Theater in Warsaw.

She started her career professionally in a play which was directed by Agnieszka Glińska called “Lulu on the Bridge”.

As of 2014 when she was still fully focused on her acting education she did a movie by Stefan Łazarski named “Parisian”.

After that, she did a lot of movies and series like “Aurora Borealis”, “First Love”, “Don’t Worry About Me”, and “Czas Honoru”.

Also in 2017, Zofia Domalik had her top-rated movie called “End of View”. Later, she did a lot of movies like “Letters from Russia” (TV show)- 2017, “Miasto 44” – 2014, and “Time of Honor” (TV series) – 2011 which bought her fame.

Awards and Nominations

Zofia Domalik has received many awards. She received an award for her debut acting in a movie called All for My Mother at the 44th Polish Film Festival held in Gdynia.

Domalik was also nominated for Eagles 2021 award for the Discovery of the Year and the best leading female role categories.
