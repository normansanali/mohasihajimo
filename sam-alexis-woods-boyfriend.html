Is Tiger Woods daughter dating?

Sam Alexis Woods is the daughter of professional golfer Tiger Woods and his ex-wife Elin Nordegren.

On Wednesday, March 9, 2022, Sam Alexis went viral for her speech at her father’s induction into the World Golf Hall of Fame.

Sam Alexis Woods boyfriend: Is Tiger Woods daughter dating?

The answer to this is no. Even though the celebrity child has been in the spotlight since she was a baby, there hasn’t been any information on any potential boyfriends.

Who is Sam Alexis Woods’ mother?

Her mother is Elin Nordegren, the daughter of Swedish former Swedish migration and asylum policy minister, Barbro Holmberg.

She has a twin sister, Josefin, and an older brother Axel.

Nordegren was introduced to Tiger Woods by Swedish golfer Jesper Parnevik after being hired by his wife as an Au Pair. Though she was initially uninterested in Woods, the pair ended up dating and getting married in October 2004.

After two kids and Woods’ admission of infidelity, the couple got divorced in August 2010. She received a $100 million settlement from the divorce.

In October 2019, Nordegren gave birth to a son who was fathered by former National Football League player Jordan Cameron.
