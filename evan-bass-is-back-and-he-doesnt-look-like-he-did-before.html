Evan Bass is back And he doesnt look like he did before

Bachelor Nation fans haven’t heard from Evan Bass much… until recently. He has been speaking out about his divorce from Carly Waddell this past week.

While Evan kept quiet for about two years, he has revealed how hard the divorce has been for him and that he is still saddened by it.

As Evan has stated, he thinks he and Carly’s relationship didn’t work out because they didn’t have a proper foundation built, but they have still done a good job co-parenting. Because, as Evan has said, it’s all about the kids at this point.

Evan hasn’t been in the spotlight much since the split, but now he seems to be talking about the divorce and putting himself back out there, which is especially evident in his newest social media photo that fans can’t stop talking about.

Evan Bass’ comeback photo is nearly unrecognizable

This past week, Evan has seemed to reappear in the headlines, and yesterday, his Instagram story photo shocked everyone.

As he took a selfie while taking a run in Boston, Bachelor Nation immediately noticed his muscular upper body in his thin tank top, which is a new look for him.

He also looked to be graying a bit on the sides of his hair, which made him look more distinguished, along with his shortened goatee.

On the @bachelor_dishes_the_dirt Instagram page, they took Evan’s photo and inserted a fire flame emoji, as well as a cloud raining in the top corner. They also captioned it, “Evan is back… that’s all I’m going to say… “ It was paired with a red rose and a sweating emoji.

Evan himself and others commented on his hot, new look

Evan was the first to comment on the uploaded photo they took from his own story, as he simply put two kissing face emojis to show his love for the repost.

Another fan wrote, “@theebass Did you have a serious glow-up?? Like you were always fine but now you are Dayuuuuummmm fine. I know I’m not the only one seeing this?? (three fire flame emojis).”

Others commented on Evan’s new look and muscular body as they exclaimed things like, “Woah,” “Whoaaaa,” and “I’m shook.”

One other claimed, “Some protein shakes have been enjoyed I think. That is a pleasant turn of events. Good for you Evan.”

While Evan has focused on his kids the past couple of years since his split from Carly, he has clearly also spent time on himself and his own nutrition, well-being, and workout plans.

Bachelor in Paradise premieres Tuesday, September 27 on ABC.
