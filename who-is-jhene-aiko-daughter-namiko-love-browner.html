Who is Jhen Aiko daughter Namiko Love Browner?

Aiko Jhené Jhené Aiko, best known by her stage name Jhené, is an American singer-songwriter and recording artist who was born in Los Angeles, California.

On March 16, 1988, Efuru Chilombo was born. She signed deals with Epic Records and The Ultimate Group before leaving to finish her degree.

Aiko is not connected to the rapper “Lil’ Fizz,” despite the fact that she began her career as his cousin in the music industry. Aiko claims that she and Lil’ Fizz were close, growing up together and that they were like family, but Sony and Epic utilized this as a marketing gimmick to highlight and promote her through the R&B group B2K.

The American music producer No I.D.-run Artium record label, which is distributed by Def Jam Recordings, and Aiko inked a recording agreement on December 16, 2011. Her voice has been characterized as soft and pop-influenced.

Who is Jhené Aiko’s daughter Namiko Love Browner?

On November 19, 2008, Namiko Browner was born in Los Angeles, California. Her acting credits include Jhené Aiko: The Pressure (2014) and Jhené Aiko: Eternal Sunshine (2015). Namiko Love Browner, Jhené’s daughter, was born when she was 20 years old.
