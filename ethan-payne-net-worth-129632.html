Ethan Payne Net Worth

Ethan Payne is a popular YouTube comedic video game commentator from England, born on June 20, 1995. He has two channels, Behzinga and Beh2inga, which feature videos of games such as FIFA and Grand Theft Auto. His channels have amassed a total of over 4 million subscribers.

About

Comedic video game commentator on YouTube who publishes videos featuring games ranging from FIFA to Grand Theft Auto on his channels Behzinga and Beh2inga. He has over 4 million total subscribers between the two channels. 

Before Fame

He started his original channel Behzinga in February of 2012 to post videos just about FIFA. He then created his second channel in August of 2013 to post about various other games. 

Trivia

He is a member of the YouTube Gaming crew Ultimate Sidemen. He's expressed interest in studying game design. 

Family Life

He resides in London, England. He featured his mother in the February 2015 video "MY MUM OPENS SPECIAL PACKS!" He has featured his girlfriend and blogger Emily Sarah Geere on his channel.

Associated With

He is joined in Ultimate Sidemen by Wroetoshaw and KSI, among others.
