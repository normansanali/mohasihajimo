Carlos Alcaraz emulated Jude Bellingham as he celebrated victory in the US Open quarter-finals.

The reigning champion beat Alexander Zverev in straight sets to tee up a semi-final clash with Daniil Medvedev at Flushing Meadows.

Putting on a clinical display, Alcaraz was clearly inspired by his club's newest blockbuster arrival.

A die-hard Real Madrid supporter, the 20-year-old has been watching on in awe as Bellingham takes to life at the Santiago Bernabeu like a duck to water.

As a result, Alcaraz dedicated his victory to Los Blancos' new star by emulating his now-iconic celebration in front of supporters in New York.

Alcaraz spoke of his admiration for Bellingham in a press conference last week, saying: "I'm really happy to watch Jude Bellingham play at Real Madrid.
