Some Lesser Known Facts About Dimple Kapadia

  • Does Dimple Kapadia smoke?: Not Known
  • Does Dimple Kapadia drink alcohol?: Yes
  • She was born in a Gujarati family.
  • Her father, Chunnibhai Kapadia, was a Gujarati entrepreneur.
  • Her younger sister, Simple Kapadia, was also an actress and died of cancer in 2009.
  • At the age of 15, she was married to Rajesh Khanna 6 months before the release of her debut film, Bobby, in 1973.
  • She retired from acting for 12 years after her first film. According to sources, it was Rajesh Khanna who put a ban on her acting after the marriage.
  • She considers her marriage to Rajesh Khanna a farce.
  • In an interview, she revealed that she is a “film-crazy” and had aspired to become an actress when she was a child.
  • Raj Kapoor introduced her to film industry and gave her a lead role in the 1973 teen romance- Bobby.
  • She became the first Indian Actress to flaunt a bikini in her debut film- Bobby.
  • Her performance in the film, Bobby, is considered 4th-best female debut of all-time in Hindi Cinema. She also received Filmfare Award for the Best Actress for Bobby.
  • After a gap of 12 years, she made a comeback in 1984 with the film Saagar, which became a sensational hit. It was also selected as India’s official entry to the Oscars in 1985.
  • In 1994, she won National Film Award for Best Actress for the film Rudaali (1993).
  • She enjoys a great rapport with her son-in-law Akshay Kumar and in an interview, Kumar revealed that his best friend is his mother-in-law.
  • In September 2017, Dimple and Sunny Deol Sunny Deol, whose relationship in the 1980s has been an open secret, were spotted at a bus stop in London holding each other’s hands.
