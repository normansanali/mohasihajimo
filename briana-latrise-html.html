Briana Latrise - Age, Family, Bio

Photographer and blogger who founded Tuesdays At Heart. She became the strategic consultant for With Love Productions and wrote the television series The People in the Back. 

Before Fame

She became known as the step daughter of singer Mary J. Blige. 

Trivia

She was the event producer for D F Enterprises.

Family Life

She is the eldest daughter of record executive Kendu Isaacs.

Associated With

Her father originally became Mary J. Blige's manager.
