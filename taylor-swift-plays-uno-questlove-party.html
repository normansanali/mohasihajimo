Taylor Swift Plays Uno In Rare Photos of Her Downtime With Friends

On Friday, Questlove shared a bunch of photos from another Uno night party that included quite a few of his fellow celebs. In a series of candid pictures, Taylor Swift made an appearance, giving fans a rare glimpse into how she spends her downtime. The musical artist recently wrapped up her U.S. leg of the Eras Tour, and looked like she was enjoying a chill night with friends.

In one pic, she is seated next to backup singer Melanie Nyema and across from Channing Tatum as they play the popular card game together. Swift is smiling over her shoulder and holding a bowl and spoon with some snacks inside.

More photos were posted by comedian Cathy Rong, who shared another of image of Swift from a Polaroid where she appears to be leaning in to hear some really good gossip.

Other celebrities to make an appearance include Jason Sudeikis, Gigi Hadid, Cara Delevingne, and Trevor Noah.

Swift was also at an Uno game night hosted by Questlove earlier this summer. The musician shared photos of that event on July 13, writing, “I’m always having Movie Night, Food Salons, Jam Sessions, Karaoke Madness anything communal with my peers et all...I am Fun Dude Quest.”

At that event, guests included Swift, Sudeikis, Nyema, Michael Che, and Mike Birbiglia.

The array of games played included “Uno Dare Adults Only,” “Uno House Rules,” “Giant Uno” and “Uno Flip!”

Swift has already had a busy weekend, attending the rehearsal dinner for good friend Jack Antonoff's upcoming wedding to actress Margaret Qualley in New Jersey on Friday night. Fans flocked to take a picture of the star entering the restaurant for the event, causing police to arrive and help direct traffic.
