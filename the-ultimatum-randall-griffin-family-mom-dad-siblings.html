Who Is Randall Griffin Dad, Randal Griffin Sr?

Randall Griffin was born to his father Randall Griffin Sr.

Born in February 1962, Randall Sr is currently 60 years old. Based on Randall Sr.’s LinkedIn, he is a professional musician and a music studio owner. He founded After Life Recording in 1972 and has been running the company for over 50 years.

Furthermore, OpenCorporates revealed that he founded Griffin Development Consulting LLC in 2005. The company is based in Heathrow Dr, Riverdale, Georgia. He finalized the company’s dissolution in December 2015.

Meanwhile, dun&bradstreet shared that the company specialized in the manufacturing of Lawn Hose Nozzles And Sprinklers. The annual revenue of the company was reported to be around $94,000.

Coming to his education, Randall Sr. earned his BA degree in Accounting from Tuskegee. While there, he was a member of Tuskegee Institute Golden Tigers Marching and Concert Bands. You can find him on Facebook (@randall.griffin.315).

Who Is Randall Griffin Mom, Devlin Griffin?

Born on 30 April 1961, Devlin Taylor Griffin is the mother of Randall Griffin. Per her date of birth, she is currently 60 years old.

By profession, Devlin is a nurse. According to OpenGovUS, she received her Public Health Nurse license on 11 September 1986. Whereas, she earned her license to work as a Registered Nurse in 1984.

Formerly Devlin worked at Santa Monica Hospital as a Registered Nurse starting in October 1986. Starting from 2020, she has been working at Fulton County Government.

Devlin also advocates breast cancer awareness.
