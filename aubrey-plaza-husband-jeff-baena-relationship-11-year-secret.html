Aubrey Plaza's Relationship With Husband Jeff Baena Has Been Her Closely Guarded Secret For 11 Years

Highlights

  • Aubrey Plaza surprised fans with her last-minute wedding to long-term boyfriend Jeff Baena.
  • The couple had a quick ceremony facilitated by a service called 1hourmarriage.com.
  • Jeff Baena is a screenwriter and director, and he and Plaza have worked together on several projects.

Before Aubrey Plaza came out as bisexual in 2016, she almost got married in Las Vegas to Michael Cera during their secret relationship. Despite her typically reserved approach to discussing relationships on social media, Plaza was in a long-term relationship with Jeff Baena.

However, in May 2021, Plaza surprised her fans when she revealed that she was already married in an Instagram post. Plaza's revelation about her last-minute wedding on The Ellen Show to her long-term boyfriend caught many off guard, as they were unaware of her relationship with Baena.

Inside Aubrey Plaza And Jeff Baena's Secret Relationship

In a GQ interview in November 2022, Aubrey Plaza revealed that she and Jeff Baena had quietly tied the knot in 2020 through a quick ceremony. The surprise marriage announcement came in 2021 when Plaza shared the news on Instagram where she posted a photo captioned,

"So proud of my darling husband @jeffbaena for dreaming up another film that takes us to Italia to cause some more trouble."

Baena and Plaza first crossed paths in 2011, though the specific details of their initial meeting and the early days of their relationship remain private. Although Plaza and Baena maintained privacy about their 12-year relationship, glimpses from interviews and social media hinted at an adorable coupling.

What Does Aubrey Plaza’s Husband, Jeff Baena, Do?

Aubrey Plaza's husband, Jeff Baena, works as a screenwriter and director with over a dozen credits to his name. Baena marked his directorial debut by writing and directing the zombie comedy Life After Beth, starring Plaza.

Baena shared with Seensome in a 2014 interview:

"It's all I ever want to do; it felt really comfortable and fun. I've been a writer for twelve years now, and it was always a means to an end. I never wanted to be a writer; I wanted to be a director, so this was always my intention, and it felt right."

Baena also created the anthology series Cinema Toast, which aired on Showtime in 2021 and served as Plaza's directorial debut. His 2022 project, Spin Me Round, was a comedy film featuring Plaza, Alison Brie, Debby Ryan, and Molly Shannon.

Did Aubrey Plaza Work With Her Husband, Jeff Baena, Before Getting Married?

Jeff Baena is a long-time collaborator of Aubrey Plaza and has worked alongside her on several projects. Baena emphasized the rarity of having the opportunity to engage in fulfilling creative projects together with Plaza, "She's awesome. I'd be working with her if she wasn't my wife, but luckily she's my wife."

According to People, Plaza acknowledged the potential challenges of working in showbiz, but they maintained balance by pursuing their individual endeavors. Plaza told Cosmo the importance of collaboration in a relationship:

"I think collaboration is key. You have to create a really safe space to work together, because you're the one person that can really offend your partner, and you're the one person that they want approval from the most, and then you're also the one person that could, like, take them down so hard-core… I don't want to compete with my partner. I want to compete with everyone else."

Plaza discussed their relationship while drawing parallels between herself and her White Lotus character, in a November 2022 GQ Magazine interview. She expressed a long-standing desire for children, revealing considerations for parenthood during the pandemic.
