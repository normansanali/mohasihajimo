How Old Is Alex Rodriguez?

The straightforward answer to the question about Alex Rodriguez’s age is simple; he was born on July 27, 1975. Born in Manhattan to a father identified as Victor Rodriguez and a mother revealed to be Lourdes Rodriguez, the former baseball star spent his early days in different places; he spent his first four years in Manhattan then relocated to The Dominican Republic with his parents at the age of 4 before returning to America and settling down in Miami, Florida.

Alex Rodriguez who is fondly called A-Rod by his many fans developed an interest in baseball from a young age and followed through on his passion to play the game. He played in High School and made so much buzz in the process, however, he skipped playing college baseball before he was eventually selected in the first round of the 1993 amateur draft at the age of 17 and kick-started his professional baseball career.

Rodriguez played professional baseball for 22 years in the Major League Baseball, rising to become one of the most iconic names in the sport and cementing his legacy as one of the greatest ever players of baseball. He eventually retired in 2016.

Following his retirement, he has gone ahead to make a career as a TV personality and, more recently, as a real estate investor.

Alex Rodriguez was previously married to a psychology graduate identified as Cynthia Scurtis. They got married in 2002 and had two kids together called Natasha Alexander and Ella Alexander. However, the couple divorced in 2008 after Cynthia filed for divorce, citing extra-marital affair and ’emotional abandonment’ on the part of Rodriguez.

Following their divorce, Alex Rodriguez met and fell in love with Jennifer Lopez and the two have grown stronger together ever since.

What Is The Age Difference Between Him And J.Lo?

Ever since the news of their relationship emerged, fans have been curious about how Alex Rodriguez’s age differs from that of Jennifer Lopez. This is not surprising because both Rodriguez and J.Lo are incredibly famous in their own individual rights and are bound to attract attention to themselves with their popularity. While Rodriguez is considered as one of the greatest baseball payers ever, J.Lo is also a global icon who has made her mark as an actress and singer.

Now, for the record, J.Lo was born on July 24, 1969, so she is 6 years older than Alex Rodriguez who was born on July 27, 1975. Apparently, the age difference between the couple is not a problem for them at all as their romance has grown more passionate since they started their relationships. Sources have revealed that they met in 2017 at a restaurant in New York where they were having lunch. They started a conversation and soon fell in love after finding out that they share some common interests.

After about two years of dating, Rodriguez and J.Lo announced that they are engaged in March 2019. The announcement sent fans into euphoria as millions of people sent in congratulatory messages for them on social media.
