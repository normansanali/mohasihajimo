Kristen Stewart Wears Thong in Clouds of Sils Maria Trailer: Video

Whoa there! Kristen Stewart has come a long way from playing Bella in Twilight. The 24-year-old actress bares her butt in a thong in the first offical trailer for her upcoming film, Clouds of Sils Maria.

The drama, which also stars Juliette Binoche and Chloë Grace Moretz, tells the story of a famous actress (Binoche) and her assistant (Stewart) who becomes jealous of a co-star (Moretz) and attempts to romance her boss.

In the first official trailer, released on May 22, Robert Pattinson‘s ex is seen not only trying to seduce Binoche’s character, but dozing off in a racy, black thong.
