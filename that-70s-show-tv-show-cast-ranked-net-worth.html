Here's Every Character From That '70s Show Ranked By Net Worth

When we think of sitcoms from the past, we remember Seinfeld fondly for its wit and sarcasm, we want to hang out with the gang on Friends, and maybe we were big fans of Frasier or Cheers. When we think back to the shows that we grew up watching, though, we definitely think of That 70's Show even if it's not talked about quite as much anymore.

This funny series featured a group of friends and their family members who, sure, maybe had a bit too much fun at times (cough cough)... but, hey, it was the 70's. We loved the relationships between the characters, most notably Kelso and Jackie along with Eric and Donna.

Are we curious how much money the actors who played these beloved parts have accumulated throughout their careers? Keep reading to find out the net worths of the actors on this great show.

15 Lisa Robin Kelly: The Actress Who Played Laurie Had A Net Worth Of Only $1,000 When She Died In 2013

Celebrity Net Worth says that Lisa Robin Kelly had a net worth of $1,000. This is definitely very low and it's the least amount of money that any of the cast members of That 70's Show had. She played Laurie Forman, Eric's sister, and the actress tragically passed away in 2013.

14 Josh Meyers: Seth Meyers' Younger Bro Played Randy, And He's Got A $2 Million Net Worth

Do we remember Randy from That 70's Show? He works with Hyde at the record shop, and he and Donna are romantically involved.

Celebrity Net Worth says that Josh Meyers, who played Randy (and who is the younger brother of Seth Meyers), has a net worth of $2 million. He's next up on this list.

13 Don Stark: Donna's Dad Has $5 Million In The Bank

Celebrity Net Worth says that Don Stark, who is known for playing Donna's dad Bob Pinciotti, has a net worth of $5 million.

It's interesting because that's on the lower end of the spectrum in terms of the cast members of That 70's Show... but for the rest of us, that's a huge sum of money.

12 Debra Jo Rupp: Eric's Funny Mom Kitty Has A $5 Million Net Worth

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Debra Jo Rupp's net worth is also $5 million.

We'll remember this actress for her role as Kitty Forman, Eric's mother and Red's wife. She's a sweet, funny character who has a laugh that isn't easy to forget as it's super unique. We were always happy to see her in scenes.

11 Tommy Chong: Bohemian Leo Has An $8 Million Net Worth IRL

Celebrity Net Worth says that Tommy Chong, who played Leo on the show, has a net worth of $8 million.

While many of the characters were bohemian since the show is, of course, set in the 1970s, Leo really took the cake. He had long hair and acted super chill about everything.

10 Tanya Roberts: It's $10 Million For Midge, Donna's Ditzy Mom

Donna's mom, Midge, was definitely pretty ditzy, but that was what we liked about her.

Celebrity Net Worth says that the actress who played this character, Tanya Roberts, has a net worth of $10 million. From 1980 until 1981, she appeared on the TV version of Charlie's Angels, but she hasn't done a lot of recent TV or film work.

9 Kurtwood Smith: Eric's Tough Dad Red Has $10 Million In The Bank

Eric is arguably the main character of That 70's Show, so fans saw a lot of him and his parents. Who could forget how tough and sometimes mean his dad Red was?

Celebrity Net Worth says that Kurtwood Smith, the actor who portrayed Red, also has a net worth of $10 million.

8 Topher Grace: Loveable Eric Has $10 Million

Topher Grace became famous after starring in That 70's Show, and since then, he has starred in many movies like 2004's In Good Company, 2015's American Ultra, and 2018's Delirium.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, the actor has a net worth of $10 million, which is interesting. He shares that amount with the two previous cast members.

7 Laura Prepon: Donna Has A Net Worth Of $12 Million

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Laura Prepon's net worth is $12 million. She has been in many movies since her role on That 70's Show along with starring in Orange Is The New Black.

Who could forget the romance between Eric and Laura Prepon's character Donna? Things aren't always perfect between them, but that's why it's so fun to watch them on the show.

6 Danny Masterson: The Troubled Actor Has $16 Million

In the past few years, there have been a lot of new stories about Danny Masterson, but before all of that, he was Steven Hyde on That 70's Show. He and Eric are close friends and hang out all the time.

Celebrity Net Worth says that the actor's net worth is $16 million.

5 Wilmer Valderrama: It's $20 Million For Everyone's Fave Character, Fez

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Wilmer Valderrama's net worth is $20 million.

We'll remember him as Fez, a super loveable character, but he has also become well-known for some of his high-profile romances. He has dated Demi Lovato, Mandy Moore, and Lindsay Lohan. In the past few years, he was in a few episodes of Grey's Anatomy and The Ranch.

4 Alyson Hannigan: She Played Suzy In Season 6 (Who Liked Kelso) And Now She's Got A $30 Million Net Worth

We know Alyson Hannigan from her time on Buffy The Vampire Slayer and How I Met Your Mother. She played main characters on those shows and has many fans as a result, and she was also Suzy on That 70's Show.

Celebrity Net Worth says that her net worth is $30 million.

3 Luke Wilson: Casey Kelso Has Got $50 Million

Luke Wilson is known for his role in Legally Blonde along with many other funny movies. Back in the day, he played Casey Kelso on That 70's Show, Michael Kelso's brother.

Celebrity Net Worth says that Luke Wilson's net worth is $50 million. He's definitely got one of the highest net worths of actors who were on the series.

2 Mila Kunis: The Charming Actress Who Played Jackie Has $65 Million

It's cool to think that once upon a time, Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher played characters who dated on a TV show... and now they're married with two kids.

According to Wealthy Gorilla, Mila Kunis has a net worth of $65 million. She's got the second highest net worth of the main cast members on the show.

1 Ashton Kutcher: Kelso Is Raking It In With The Highest Net Worth Of $200 Million

It makes sense that Ashton Kutcher, who is arguably the most recognizable name of anyone on That 70's Show, would also have the highest net worth.

According to Wealthy Gorilla, it's $200 million. He found fame after playing Kelso on the show, and we've loved watching him in countless movies ever since.
