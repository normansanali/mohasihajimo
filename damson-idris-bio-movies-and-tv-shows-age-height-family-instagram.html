Damson Idris Bio, Movies And TV Shows, Age, Height, Family, Instagram

Damson Idris is a British actor. He currently stars in John Singleton’s crime drama Snowfall, Idris also played the co-lead in Netflix’s sci-fi action film Outside the Wire (2021).

Born in Peckham, South-East London, he earned a BA Honours degree in Theatre, Film & Television studies.

Damson Idris Movies And TV Shows

Damson Idris has starred in many movies and tv shows including Snowfall, Astral, Farming, The Commuter, Megan Leavey, City of Tiny Lights, My Brother the Devil, Hiding in the Light, and others.

Damson Idris Age

Damson Idris is 31 years old as of 2022. He was born September 2, 1991, in Peckham, London, United Kingdom.

Damson Idris Height

Damson Idris stands at a height of 6 feet 1 inch(1.85 m) tall.

Damson Idris Family

Damson Idris is the son of Philippa Idris. He has two elder sisters and three older brothers.
