Is Thomas Brodie-Sangster In A Relationship? His Dating History, Wife

Child star, Thomas Brodie-Sangster might still look young. But the actor is already in his 30’s. Yes! And while he may have garnered credits to over 50 TV shows/movies, the charmer was more in the spotlight for dating quite a few amazing personalities — including Elon Musk’s ex-wife!

Let’s discuss his relationship and dating history below.

Is Thomas Brodie-Sangster In A Relationship With Talulah Riley?

Yes, Thomas Brodie-Sangster was in a relationship with Talulah Riley as of 2022.

Reportedly, the two began dating back in the summer of 2021 after meeting on the set of Pistols. For those of you interested, the TV mini-series was all about the Sex Pistols guitarist Steve Jones, where Thomas and Talulah portrayed Malcolm McLaren and Vivienne Westwood respectively.

But though the stars were already coupled then, it wasn’t until November that year, the world became aware of them. Also, since Talulah was in an on-off relationship with Matthew Rice then, no one doubted that the two would split for real.

“Talulah was dating Matthew right up until the spring, and he’s still a little shocked and saddened it has all ended between them,” a source shared.

The last we saw Thomas and Talulah was in Feb 2022, out to lunch at the Cleethorpes Pier. But that wasn’t the only time the duo went out for a chippie. Just a month before, the couple made it to Papas, for that delicious fish and chips which cost just £8.99.

Well, considering Thomas was dating the ex-wife of the world’s richest person, it was one hell of a bargain.

For those of you who don’t know, Talulah and Elon Musk were married from 2010 to 2012, and 2013 to 2014. Yes! They were married and divorced twice. She is Elon’s second wife. Previously, the SpaceX founder was married to Justine Musk for eight long years until 2008.

Thomas Brodie-Sangster Dating History

Thomas Brodie-Sangster’s last known girlfriend before Talulah was the IG star, photographer, and model, Gzi Wisdom. The two made their relationship IG official on Valentine’s Day 2019. (Alprazolam) However, reports had that the pair were together since 2016.

Since making it official, the duo often graced events together including Raindance Film Festival and The Gentleman’s Journal Summer Party. Furthermore, they even appeared in a music video together. It was on a British band Hotel Lux’s track “Ballad Of You & I.”

The two were last seen together in 2020. So, that was probably the year, Thomas and Gzi went their separate ways.

Before Gzi, Thomas was dating the former child actress, Isabella Melling. The duo started their relationship in December 2012 and also went on to make several public appearances together including the one at the 2016 London Critics’ Circle Film Awards.

Sadly, their relationship came to an end in 2016.

As for Thomas’ earliest relationship, rumors had that the actor dated the designer Patience Harding from 2008 to 2009. However, there was never enough evidence to confirm the matter.

Has Thomas Brodie-Sangster Ever Been Married In The Past?

No, Thomas Brodie-Sangster was never married in the past before dating Talulah Riley.

This never aging actor was so dedicated to his profession, he never thought about settling down. However, he did try his shots at love, only to fail each time.

Talking about his dedication, Thomas once even leaned to play-guitar left-handed for his role as “Paul” in Nowhere Boy (2009). However, the actor already knew how to play guitar and drums (right-handed), all thanks to his father.
