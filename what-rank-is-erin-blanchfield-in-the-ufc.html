Professional Journey and Record

Erin Blanchfield, hailing from Nevada, is a formidable mixed martial arts fighter affiliated with the renowned Renzo Gracie Jiu-Jitsu association. An instutution that produced products like UFC legends Matt Serra and Georges St-Pierre.
