Who is richer Jay Leno or David Letterman?

Jay Leno is an American television host, comedian, and writer.

He was born on April 28, 1950, in New Rochelle, New York, the United States. He is 71 years old as of April 18, 2022.

He was the host of The Tonight Show (1992 to 2009).

David Michael Letterman on the other hand is also an American television host, comedian, writer, and producer.

He was born on April 12, 1947 (age 75 years), in Indianapolis, Indiana, United States.

He hosted late-night television talk shows for 33 years, beginning on February 1, 1982, with a debut of Late Night with David Letterman on NBC, and ending on May 20, 2015, broadcast of Late Show with David Letterman on CBS.

There has been an idea of Jay Leno and David Letterman hating on each other as long as the media is a concern.
