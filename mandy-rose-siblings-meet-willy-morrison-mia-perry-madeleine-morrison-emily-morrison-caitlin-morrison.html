American professional wrestler, television personality, and fitness and figure competition Amanda Rose Saccomanno was born on July 18, 1990.

She was a former NXT Women’s Champion and the leader of Toxic Attraction. She is most known for her time spent in the WWE, where she wrestled under the ring name Mandy Rose.

Mandy Rose siblings: Meet Willy Morrison, Mia Perry, Madeleine Morrison, Emily Morrison, Caitlin Morrison

Amanda had 5 siblings named Willy Morrison, Mia Perry, Madeleine Morrison, Emily Morrison, and Caitlin Morrison. Mandy and her siblings were born in the New York City suburb of Westchester County, New York. They are of Italian and Irish descent.

Richard, her older brother, passed away on October 3, 2022, following a protracted battle with depression.

She engaged in dancing while she was a student at Yorktown High School. She later graduated from Iona College with a bachelor’s degree in speech pathology. In 2013, she competed in her first fitness competition, winning the World Bodybuilding Fitness & Fashion Boston Show. She was also named the 2014 World Bikini Champion of Beauty, Fitness, and Fashion.
