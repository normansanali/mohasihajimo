Justin Bieber: Latest News and Photos

All the latest news, photos and more on Justin Bieber. The Canadian singer-songwriter shot to fame at the age of 13 after being discovered for his YouTube song covers. Since, he has gone on to achieve international stardom with over 150 million record sales worldwide with hits including Baby, Sorry and Love Yourself. Justin is married to Hailey Bieber, and has suffered from Lyme disease during the 2010s. He has received multiple awards including a Grammy, 15 American Music Awards and 21 MTV Europe Music Awards.
