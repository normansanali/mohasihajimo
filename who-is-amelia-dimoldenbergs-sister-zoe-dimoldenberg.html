Who Is Amelia Dimoldenbergs Sister Zoe Dimoldenberg?

Zoe Dimoldenberg is the only known sister of Amelia Dimoldenberg; a social media influencer, journalist, chef, and YouTuber who lives in the UK.

Zoe who hasn’t been in the limelight like her sister Amelia suddenly became an object of interest after her sister Amelia retweeted a viral post of DJ Big Smoke to reveal that they are blood sisters. Get to know more about Zoe Dimoldenberg here.

How Old is Zoe Dimoldenberg?

Zoe is 28 years old in 2023, a year younger than her sister Amelia Dimoldenberg. Even though her exact date of birth isn’t known, Amelia revealed this in a particular Chicken Shop Date she had with Central Cee.

Central Cee asked Amelia how old her sister was, to which she replied: “Year below me.” Currently, Amelia Dimoldenberg is 30 years old therefore, Zoe Dimoldenberg is 29 years old in 2023.

She was born in Westminster, England

Amelia Dimoldenberg was born to Paul Dimoldenberg and Linda Dimoldenberg in Westminster, England. She grew up in Marylebone in the City of Westminster and attended St Marylebone School alongside her elder sister. Zoe and Amelia are the only children of their parents.

So far, nothing has been revealed concerning Zoe Dimoldenberg’s further studies. However, it’s widely known that Amelia, her sister, studied A-levels in English, art, politics, and IT, and went on to obtain a Foundation Diploma in Art and Design and a BA in Fashion Communication from Central Saint Martins, London.

Is Zoe Dimoldenberg Married?

No, Zoe is not married yet. Although not much is known about her past relationships we can deduce she’s single from Amelia’s discussion with Central Cee in a particular episode of Chicken Shop Date. As stated earlier, Central Cee first asked Amelia for Zoe Dimoldenberg’s age. He then continues the conversation by casually asking if she has a boyfriend, clearly trying to pursue her sibling, but Amelia shut it down immediately.

“Why are you interested? You can’t be,” she said before asking the musician, whose real name is Oakley Neil H. T. Caesar-Su if he had met her sister. “Never met her,” he replied as Amelia hilariously quipped back: “No she’s not interested in you.”

The 53-year-old rapper continued shooting his shot and asked what her “type” is – but the host simply said: “Not you. She would not go on a date with you.” “We’re on a date, so can you stop trying to go on a date with my sister,” Amelia Dimoldenberg concluded.

Furthermore, not much can be said concerning the supposed relationship she had with Big Smoke, a movie designer, artist, and DJ. This was the whole scenario that brought Zoe Dimoldenberg into the limelight as Amelia Dimoldenberg’s sister, after Amelia retweeted Big Smoke’s tweet with a picture of Zoe on February 20, 2018.
