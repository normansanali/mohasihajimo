Who Is Joe Gatto's Wife, Bessy Gatto? Here's A Look At Their Relationship

American comedian Joe Gatto is a former star of Impractical Jokers, a television series with improvisation elements. He was married to Bessy Gatto and have two kids with her. However, they separated in 2021.

He is a famous improvisational comedian based in New York. Along with Sal Vulcano, James Murray, and Brian Quinn, he formed a comedy troupe, the Tenderloins. He starred in Impractical Jokers from its inception in 2011 to 2021.

Gatto's career in comedy began in 1998 when the four friends reunited after graduating from college. They would practice improvisation at Joe's house and went on to tour as an improv and sketch comedy troupe in 1998.

The Tenderloins began producing comedy sketches, posting them on popular social media sites such as YouTube, Myspace, and Metacafe, amassing millions of online views. In addition, they won NBC's 2007 It's Your Show competition for the sketch "Time Thugs," earning the $100,000 grand prize.

Joe Gatto And Wife Bessy Gatto's Relationship Timeline

Bessy Gatto is the former wife of comedian Joe Gatto. They tied the knots in 2013, but their marriage amicably ended in 2021.

The couple started dating in 2009 and made frequent appearances in Impractical Jokers since its debut in 2011. The two shared an excellent relationship, which Joe often shared on his social media.

Bessy used to live in California when they were first acquainted. After a couple of years, she moved to New York to live with Joe, and the couple married in 2013. 

Two years later, the couple became parents of a daughter, Milana Francis, born in 2015. Then, after a couple of years, they welcomed a son, Remington James, born in 2017.

Joe And Bessy Gatto Separated In 2021

The family of four lived happily together for a couple more years before Joe and Bessy started having differences. Then, on December 2021, the two decided to part ways, leading to Joe leaving Impractical Jokers ahead of season ten.

Joe wrote a lengthy note on Instagram that he decided to leave the reality show he had been a crucial part of for a decade. He wrote, "Bessy and I have decided to amicably part ways, so now I need to focus on being the best father and co-parent to our two incredible kids."

The news of Joe and Bessy parting ways shocked regular Impractical Jokers fans and well-wishers. Many wrote calming words for the two and wished good luck for their future.

Joe And Bessy Gatto Are Still Close To Each Other

Despite the separation, the ex-couple are still close to each other and see co-parenting their kids very well. They went to Imagine Dragon concert with their daughter in February earlier this year and seemed happy with each other.

Joe and Bessy were seen together with their two children at a September event organized by the Animal Care Center of NYC. The former Impractical Joker star was the host of the event.

Joe Gatto's Early Life

Joe Gatto was born in Staten Island, New York, on June 5, 1976. He is currently 46 years old.

He is of Italian descent, and his father passed away in 1995from unknown causes. There were rumors that he succumbed to alcoholism, although Joe has falsified the claims.

He attended Monsignor Farrell High School, where he was friends with fellow Impractical Jokers Sal Vulcano, James Murray, and Brian Quinn. Along with the Tenderloins, he was a member of his high school's Improvisation Club.

After graduating from high school, Gatto attended LIU Post and graduated with a degree in accounting.
