Is Penny Gilley related to Jimmy Swaggart?

Penny Gilley has been a singer since her teenage days. Right now, she is the hostess of a television show, The Penny Gilley show. The show started in 2017.

She is the second cousin of Mickey Gilley.

Is Penny Gilley related to Jimmy Swaggart?

Jimmy Swaggart is the cousin of the late American country music singer and songwriter, Mickey Gilley.

He is a televangelist of the Pentecost church. Jimmy Swaggart is 87 years old.

Jimmy Swaggart grew up in Ferriday, Louisiana with his parents, Willie Leon Swaggart and Minnie Bell. His father was also a preacher.

The popular televangelist has been married to Frances Anderson since he was 17 years. They have a son called Donnie.

His only sister, Jeanette Ensminger died in 1999.

In February 1988, Jimmy Swaggart was unfrocked for the accusation of being in a sex scandal with a prostitute. This was after he confessed on live television in his message, I Have Sinned.

Jimmy Swaggart and Penny Gilley are related as cousins by maternal and paternal lines.
