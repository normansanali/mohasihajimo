Tito Ortiz Net Worth

Tito Ortiz is an American mixed martial artist and former UFC Light Heavyweight Champion, born on January 23, 1975 in Huntington Beach, CA. He is best known for his long-standing rivalry with fellow MMA fighter Chuck Liddell, and for his time as a UFC champion. Ortiz has also competed in Bellator MMA and Combate Americas.
