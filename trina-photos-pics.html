Photos Of The Rapper Hollywood Life

Trina is seen at the Miami radio station’s 99 Jamz Sessions at Revolution in Fort Lauderdale on April 20, 2017. The rapper opted for a sexy black outfit featuring sheer mesh as she blew a kiss to the camera! Her glam was on point with a seductive blue glitter eye shadow, glossy lips and a bright yellow manicure.

Trina was born Katrina Laverne Taylor on December 3, 1974 in Miami, Florida. While studying to pursue real estate, Trina ended up appearing on one of Trick Daddy’s singles, which was the beginning of her music career. From there, she released her solo debut album “Da Baddest B****” in 2000, which put her on the map. She later released the hit tracks “No Panties” and “B R Right” featuring Ludacris, in addition to appearing on Kelly Rowland’s “Here We Go.”

For years, Trina has been embroiled in a feud with Philadelphia native Khia. The pair have exchanged barbs via diss tracks for nearly two decades, dating back to Khia’s claim that she wrote Trina’s first big hit.
