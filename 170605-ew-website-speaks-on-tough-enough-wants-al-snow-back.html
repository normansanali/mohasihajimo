EW Website Speaks On Tough Enough, Wants Al Snow Back

Margaret Lyons has written an article taking a look at WWE’s decision to bring "Tough Enough" back to TV. The following is an excerpt:

"They have to bring back Al Snow. A USA rep says the show is still in its really, really early phases, and that decisions about judges and mentors haven’t been made yet. Snow isn’t with the WWE anymore, so I know it’s a long shot, but still: Snow’s mentorship and training absolutely made that show. (That fourth season doesn’t exist in my world.) He was so strict and serious sometimes, and then so goofy and jovial at other — I’m not a wrestling fan, but he was so compelling that I got completely sucked into the series."

It should be noted that the article does not mention the fact that Snow is currently working for TNA, so it seems unlikely that he would be able to return to WWE to work for Tough Enough however that has not been confirmed.
