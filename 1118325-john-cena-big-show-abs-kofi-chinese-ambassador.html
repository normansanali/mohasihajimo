John Cena Inspired Big Show To Get Abs, Kofi Welcomes WWEs First Chinese Ambassador

John Cena Inspired Big Show To Get Abs

In a bonus clip from the WWE Network special Rebuilding Big Show, The Giant reveals that it was John Cena who inspired him to get fit. While the two were idly talking, Big Show stated that no one would want to see a “giant with abs.” John Cena agreed with him in a way that Big Show read as a challenge, and he bulked up just to show up the “Bumblebee” star.

Rebuilding Big Show will air in full after Smackville tomorrow night.

Kofi Welcomes WWE’s First Chinese Ambassador

WWE Champion Kofi Kingston has welcomed Chinese martial arts actor Jacky Heung into the WWE family. Jacky will serve as a representative for the company in the country, which will have a renewed focus going forward according to Kofi in this video. WWE has recently run several tryouts in China and Chinese national Xia Li competes regularly on NXT’s TV broadcasts.
