Anthony Mackie Net Worth

Anthony Mackie is an actor born on September 23, 1978 in New Orleans, LA. He is best known for his roles in the Academy Award-winning films The Hurt Locker and Million Dollar Baby. He is also well-known for his role as Sam Wilson/Falcon in the Marvel universe, appearing in films such as Captain America: The Winter Soldier and Avengers: Age of Ultron.

About

Actor who became known for his roles in the Academy Award-winning films The Hurt Locker and Million Dollar Baby. He also plays the character Sam Wilson/Falcon in the Marvel universe, appearing in such films as Captain America: The Winter Soldier and Avengers: Age of Ultron.

Before Fame

He graduated from the prestigious Juilliard School in 2001.

Trivia

He portrayed Martin Luther King Jr. in the 2016 HBO film All the Way and Tupac Shakur in the 2009 biopic film Notorious.

Family Life

He married Sheletta Chapital in 2014 and together they have three children. He has a brother named Calvin.

Associated With

He made his feature film debut opposite Eminem in the 2002 film 8 Mile.
