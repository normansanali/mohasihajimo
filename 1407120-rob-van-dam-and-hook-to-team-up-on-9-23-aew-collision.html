Rob Van Dam And HOOK To Team Up On 9/23 AEW Collision

A buzzworthy tag team match has been added to the September 23 episode of AEW Collision.

As confirmed during AEW Rampage: Grand Slam, WWE Hall of Famer Rob Van Dam and FTW Champion HOOK will team up on Saturday’s episode of AEW Collision. They will face “Daddy Magic” Matt Menard and “Cool Hand Ang” Angelo Parker. This will be Rob Van Dam’s second AEW match. In his debut, he lost to Jack Perry on the August 9 episode of AEW Dynamite.

Plus, Julia Hart will take on Willow Nightingale. Hart beat Skye Blue on AEW Rampage, and Willow came out for the save when she attacked Blue after the bell.

9/23 AEW Collision Card

The updated card for the September 23 episode of AEW Collision is as follows:

  • : Ricky Starks vs. Bryan Danielson
  • Rob Van Dam returns to the ring
  • : FTR (Dax Harwood & Cash Wheeler) vs. The WorkHorsemen (JD Drake & Anthony Henry)
  • Jay White vs. Andrade El Idolo
  • : Luchasaurus (c) vs. Darby Allin vs. Christian Cage
  • Rob Van Dam & HOOK vs. Matt Menard & Angelo Parker
  • Julia Hart vs. Willow Nightingale

WrestleZone will have coverage of AEW Collision as it airs on Saturday.
