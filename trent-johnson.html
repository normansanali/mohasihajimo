Biography

Trent Johnson was born in California on September 12, 1956. In 2012, he became the coach of Texas Christian University’s Horned Frogs Division I basketball team. Previously, he had served as head basketball coach at Louisiana State University, Stanford University, and the University of Nevada, Reno. He and fellow college basketball coach Tubby Smith both coached Southeastern Conference (SEC), tournament-winning teams. He played his college basketball career at Boise State University and, in 1983, earned an undergraduate degree in physical education.
