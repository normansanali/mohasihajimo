Prince William &amp; Kate: The Prince &amp; Princess of Wales Latest News

Prince William met Kate Middleton when they were both students at the University of St Andrews and the pair began dating in 2003. The couple announced their engagement in November 2010 and were married at Westminster Abbey in April 2011. William and Kate are parents to Prince George, Princess Charlotte and Prince Louis. They are senior members of the royal family and carry out engagements for their patronages.
