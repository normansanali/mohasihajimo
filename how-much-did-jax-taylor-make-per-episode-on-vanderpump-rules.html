How Much Did Jax Taylor Make Per Episode On Vanderpump Rules?

As far as salaries go, Jax Taylor, an original "Vanderpump Rules" cast member, was sitting pretty at the top of the heap. Well, almost. According to InTouch, Taylor was raking in $25,000 by the time he left the successful reality show. Aside from queen bee, Lisa Vanderpump, who makes around $50,000 per episode, Taylor's salary equaled or surpassed all of his other cast members. Stylecaster described Taylor as "the second richest cast member," citing Celebrity Net Worth and their valuation of his $2 million net worth. Stassi Schroeder and Kristen Doute made $25,000 per episode prior to their firing, as well. Meanwhile, Kate Maloney and Tom Swartz made between $10,000 and $25,000. 

Though Taylor can no longer depend on Bravo to line his pockets, he's a man of many trades who has more than one source of income. According to Life&Style, Taylor has his hands in the modeling, influencer, and real estate world.
