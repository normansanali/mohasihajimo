NFL: Calvin Ridley's Wife - Is He Married Or Still Dating Girlfriend Dominique Fitchard

Calvin Ridley of the Atlanta Falcons is in a happy relationship with his wife, Dominique Fitchard.

The couple began dating in high school and is said to have been together for a long time. Their Instagram posts demonstrate their unwavering support and concern for one another, demonstrating to fans how fortunate they are to have each other.

According to reports, Calvin proposed to Fitchard on the occasion of her birthday in 2019. And the couple has a lovely daughter, Cree, who is four years old. However, they do not appear to be married at this time.

Furthermore, Calvin’s wedding is not mentioned on his social media. Perhaps they’ll plan their big day when they’re ready.

Dominique Fitchard was born on February 9, 1993, and is 29 years old. She grew up in Huntsville, Alabama, and has a twin sister named Monique Fitchard.

She attended Johnson High School and played varsity basketball there. She then went to Tuskegee University and obtained her undergraduate degree there.

She is active on Instagram, with over 31K followers and over 114 posts. On Instagram, she frequently posts about her daily life, business, and personal life.

Dominique revealed their pregnancy on Instagram in February 2020 with a stunning baby bump. On June 27, 2020, at 9:59 p.m., they welcomed the sweet little girl. Both parents express their love for their daughter on Instagram. They are raising her as if she is a world-conquering princess.
