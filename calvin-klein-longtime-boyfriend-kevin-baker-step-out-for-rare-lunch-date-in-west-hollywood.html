Calvin Klein &amp; Longtime Boyfriend Kevin Baker Step Out for Rare Lunch Date in West Hollywood

Calvin Klein is enjoying a very rare lunch date with his longtime boyfriend Kevin Baker.

The 79-year-old fashion designer and the 34-year-old model made their way through the parking lot after grabbing lunch at Mauro’s Cafe on Sunday afternoon (June 19) in West Hollywood, Calif.

For their outing, Calvin sported a gray shirt, jeans, and aviator sunglasses while Kevin wore an olive green-colored shirt and tan trousers.

Calvin and Kevin made their first public appearance together back in 2016 when they walked the red carpet together at the American Ballet Theatre’s Fall Gala.

The two of them have never publicly addressed their relationship, but there have been reports about their romance for years.

The last time Calvin and Kevin were spotted together was at the same restaurant back in December 2021.
