Ryan Trahan Height, Weight, Age, Girlfriend, Biography, Family &amp; More

Ryan Trahan (born 7 October 1998) is an American YouTuber, Internet Personality, Vlogger, Athlete, Entrepreneur, Runner, and Instagram Celeb from Texas. He is known for his Youtube videos, where he upload content related to his daily lifestyle, fun stuff, and reviews. In addition, he also gave his athletic training tips to his subscribers.

As a result, he gained fame and earned more than 1.5+ million followers on his self-titled Youtube channel.

The 20-year-old has also founded 2 companies like Neptune Bottle, and Hydra Collective. Furthermore, Neptune Bottle has raised more than $30,921 US Dollars funds on the Kickstarter website. The primary goal of this project is to manufacture quality reusable bottles which will reduce plastic pollution across the globe.

On the other hand, the Hydra Collective is an eCommerce site that sells T-shirts, hoodies, stickers, and other clothing stuff.
