What is Chip and Joanne Gaines' net worth?

CHIP and Joanna Gaines' have been America's fixer-uppers since 2013 and became a household name after the release of their HGTV show.

The hosts of HGTV's, Fixer Upper, showed viewers the process of turning run-down homes into stunning masterpieces that revitalize whole neighborhoods through their remodeling and design company, Magnolia Homes.

What is Chip and Joanne Gaines' net worth?

Individually, Chip, 47, and Joanna, 43, each has an estimated net worth of $10million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Together, the home repair team has an estimated $20 million, most of which comes from their HGTV show Fixer Upper.

In 2019, the couple launched a joint venture with HGTV that involves them having a TV network and streaming app of their own, called Magnolia Network, which launched in January 2022.

How did Chip and Joanne Gaines meet?

Chip and Joanne's love story dates back to 2001 and began after Chip saw a photo of her on the wall at her father’s tire shop.

“Her dad made the mistake of putting a pic of the family behind the counter at his [automotive] shop. I knew I’d marry her one day just by the picture on the wall," Chip told Popsugar.
