Olivia Holt Wiki Biography

Born Olivia Hastings Holt on the 5th August 1997, in Germantown, Tennessee USA, she is an actress and singer, best known to the world as Skylar Lewis in the television film “Girl Vs. Monster”, and as Kim Crawford in the TV series “Kickin’ It”, while regarding her musical endeavors, she is known for her single – “History”.

Have you ever wondered how rich Olivia Holt is, as of early 2018? According to authoritative sources, it has been estimated that Holt’s net worth is as high as $1 million, an amount earned through her successful career, active since 2009.

Olivia Holt Net Worth $1 Million

One of three children born to Kim and her husband Mark Holt, Olivia spent her first years in Germantown, but then her family moved to Nesbit, Mississippi. From an early age, Olivia was drawn to the performing arts, and aside from gymnastic lessons, Olivia also began honing her acting skills in local theater productions, but also in commercials for such products as Kidz Bop 14, Bratz Dolls, Hasbro, and others, so establishing her net worth.

She made her screen debut in 2009 with a minor role in the film “Black and Blue”, while two years later, she was chosen for the part of Kim Crawford in Disney’s TV series “Kickin’ It”. The role launched her to stardom, and from then on, Olivia’s career began a constant rise. In 2012 she was chosen for the lead role of Skylar Lewis in the television film “Girl Vs. Monster”, while after several successful years in “Kickin’ It”, she left the show and joined another Disney series – “I Didn’t Do It” (2014-2015) – as Lindy Watson, further increasing her net worth.

Then in 2016, Olivia starred in the comedy film “The Standoff”, next to Alex Wolf and Sydney Park, while the following year Olivia had a supporting role in the romantic comedy film “Class Rank”, featuring Kristin Chenoweth, Bruce Dern, and Skyler Gisondo in the lead roles. Most recently, Oliva has been working on the action-adventure series “Cloak & Dagger”, and the fantasy comedy film “Status Update”, both scheduled for release late in 2018,.

Aside from acting, Olivia is also an accomplished musician; her musical career started in 2011 when she recorded three songs for the film “Girl Vs. Monster”. In 2014 she signed a contract with Hollywood Records, and released her debut single two years later – “Phoenix”. The same year, Olivia also released her EP “Olivia”, which reached No. 19 on the US Top Heatseekers. In support of her release, Olivia embarked on a tour, joining Ryland Lynch, Forever in Your Mind, and Isaac Elliot, which also added to her net worth.

Most recently, Olivia collaborated with Brandon Beal on the song “Paradise”, and with MYBADD on the song “Party on a Weekday”, while she also released another single “Generous”.

Olivia has become a brand ambassador for Neutrogena, and has also partnered with mobile gaming system maker PlayMG, which also contributed to her wealth.

Regarding her personal life, Olivia dated Ray Kearin for two years, before breaking up with him in 2017. According to sources, she is now in a relationship with actor Taylor Lautner.
