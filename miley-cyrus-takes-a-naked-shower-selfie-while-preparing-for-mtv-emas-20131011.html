Miley Cyrus Shares Naked Shower Selfie: Pic Before MTV EMAs

Miley Cyrus is getting ready for the MTV European Music Awards (EMAs) stage in Amsterdam, but first things first! The selfie-happy star decided to share her EMA prep with her more than 15 million Twitter followers. 

"I got that shower time showerrrrr time selfiiiie. (A la lana del ray) #happyemas," she captioned the sultry pic. 

Though she's known at this point for baring literally all (see: her "Wrecking Ball" video and shocking Terry Richardson photo shoot), Cyrus, 20, kept her shower scene pretty tame, only showing off her bare shoulders and chain necklaces. To clean up, Cyrus wore her hair in buns similar to the style she sported during her now-infamous 2013 VMA performance at Brooklyn's Barclays Center back in August.
