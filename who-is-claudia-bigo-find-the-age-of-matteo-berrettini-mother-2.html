Who Is Claudia Bigo? Find The Age Of Matteo Berrettini Mother

Claudia Bigo is the mother of Italian professional tennis player, Matteo Berrettini. Learn more about the tennis giant’s mother including her age.

Claudia Bigo is the mother of Matteo Berrettini who is an Italian professional tennis player. He is currently seeded number 9 player in ATP World rankings and has a career-high of number 8 in singles ranking.

Claudia is a proud mother of two boys, Matteo, and his younger brother, Jacopo. Jacopo is following in his older brother’s footsteps to become a professional player himself.

Claudia Bigo Age: How Old Is Matteo Berrettini Mother?

Matteo Berrettini’s mother Claudia Bigo’s age is unknown. She has yet to reveal her age to the public.

There is also no information on the web about Claudia’s age or date of birth. She might be somewhere between 50-55 years old. However, we cannot be certain about the age as she has not shared it anywhere.

Claudia and her husband, Luca Berrettini, both are very proud parents. According to Matteo’s Wikipedia page, Luca is originally from Florence, Italy whereas Claudia’s mother is from Rio de Janeiro in Brazil.

The family is currently living in Rome, Italy but Matteo has recently moved to Monaco for which he even has been criticized. He along with others few have been criticized for moving their residency for financial benefits.
