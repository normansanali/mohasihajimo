Daisuke Kishio

Daisuke Kishio (Japanese: 岸尾大輔 Kishio Daisuke) is a Japanese voice actor for the Pokémon anime. He was born on March 28, 1974 in Matsusaka, Mie Prefecture, Japan.

He is a member of Tokyo Actor's Consumer's Cooperative Society, and has been nominated for "Best Actor in a Leading role", "Best Actor in a Supporting Role", and "Best Radio Personality" after the second voice actor Awards.

Pokémon roles

Kishio has had various roles throughout the series, mainly voicing characters of the day.
