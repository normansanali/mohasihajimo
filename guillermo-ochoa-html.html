Memo Ochoa - Age, Family, Bio

Mexican goalkeeper who joined Ajaccio in 2011 and became a member of Mexico's national team in 2005. He's also played for Málaga and Granada. In August 2019, he returned to Club América where he had previously played for seven years. 

Before Fame

He began his professional soccer career playing with the Mexican team Club America at the age of 19.

Trivia

He was named the 2000-2010 Best Mexican Goalkeeper by the International Federation of Football History.

Family Life

His full name is Francisco Guillermo Ochoa Magaña. He married Karla Mora and he has two daughters and a son. He previously dated actress Dulce María. 

Associated With

He became teammates with Javier Hernández on the Mexican national team in 2009.
