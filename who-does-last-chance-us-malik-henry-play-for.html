Who does Last Chance U's Malik Henry play for?

Malik Henry, the former Nevada quarterback and star of the Netflix show Last Chance U, currently plays for the Frisco Fighters in the Indoor Football League. Henry, a former four-star recruit, faced challenges throughout his college football career, including suspensions and transfers, which made it difficult for him to find stability. His brief stint at Nevada in 2019 saw him playing three games before being removed from participation to focus on academics and life off the field. Despite this, he is now listed as one of three quarterbacks on the Frisco Fighters' roster, although he has yet to play a game this season. The Fighters, led by coach Clint Dolezel, currently boast a 2-0 record in the 14-game regular season.

Henry's journey was prominently featured in the third and fourth seasons of Netflix's Last Chance U, where his struggles and aspirations were showcased. In an interview, Henry expressed a desire for the show to depict his close relationship with coach Jason Brown, highlighting the importance of their bond during his time at Independence Community College.

After transferring from Independence Community College to Nevada in 2019, Henry's performance at Nevada was mixed. Despite making two starts, he completed 53.8 percent of his attempts for 593 yards with one touchdown and four interceptions. These statistics reflect the challenges he faced in securing a starting position and making a significant impact on the field. Furthermore, Henry is no longer enrolled at the University of Nevada, marking another chapter in his journey as he transitions to the professional arena with the Frisco Fighters.

The Frisco Fighters, based in Frisco, Texas, provide Henry with a new opportunity to showcase his talent and determination in the Indoor Football League. Coached by Clint Dolezel, the team offers an environment where Henry can continue to develop his skills and contribute to their success on the field. While his playing time this season has been limited, Henry's presence on the roster signifies his ongoing pursuit of a successful football career beyond the challenges he faced in college.

The Indoor Football League serves as a platform for players like Henry to continue pursuing their football dreams. With a focus on providing exciting and competitive football action, the league offers a stage for athletes to display their abilities and potentially transition to higher levels of professional football.
